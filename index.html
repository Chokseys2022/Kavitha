About Catering


Cooking since 2000

Kavitha's Catering was founded by Kavitha in 1995 with a simple mission: to provide exceptional culinary experiences for all kinds of events. With a passion for fine food and a commitment to quality, Kavitha started the company with the vision of offering a personalized approach to catering, where every dish is crafted to perfection.
